New Staff Cabin

We are excited to announce that we are embarking on a new project to build a four-unit cabin that will house our summer staff as well as other volunteers that come to Dayspring to assist with programming and maintenance throughout the year. This new facility will provide comfortable and safe living quarters for our staff members / volunteers who play a vital role in the ongoing ministry at Dayspring.

Here’s another exciting bit of information…. Each of these cabins will not only be climate controlled, but they will have their own private bathroom (complete with showers)! Not only will this structure allow us to have additional room for staff, but it will serve as a significant impact our increasing camp rental program (from which all funds go to provide a means for children to attend camp with no set cost).

We have another bit of exciting news - there is a group of generous donors who have a greed to match donations dollar for dollar for the first $5,000 raised. This means that your donation will be doubled and go twice as far toward the goal of constructing this project. All funds will be used toward the purchase of materials as there are several faithful volunteers, who give of their time to ensure that the labor to construct this project is provided at no cost.

We need your help to make this project a reality. We are seeking donations from individuals, businesses, and organizations who believe in the mission of Dayspring Bible Camp and the impact it has on the lives of the children and families who attend. No donation is too small, and all contributions are tax-deductible.

With your support, we can provide our staff with comfortable accommodations and continue to provide an outstanding summer camp experience for children where they can hear the gospel of Jesus Christ - the “good news” of salvation. Thank you in advance for your generosity and support.
